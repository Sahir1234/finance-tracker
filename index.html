My Online Finance Tracker!

(That name is only temporary)

This application lets you track your expenses over time. You can sign up with an account using a username and a password. Whenever, you make a purchase, you can login to the homepage and add it to your "journal". You can also see a graph of your spending over time and your individual entries. Hopefully, this helps you keep track of your expenses.
