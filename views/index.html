My Online Finance Tracker!


This application lets you track your expenses over time. You can sign up with an account and then whenever you make a purchase or even make money, you can login to the homepage and add it to your "journal". You can also see a graph of your spending over time and your individual entries. If necessary, you can even edit or delete entries. Hopefully, this helps you keep track of your expenses and be more financially aware.


If you wish to contribute to this project or have any suggestions, please contact the developer at sahir.mody@gmaill.com. Thanks!
